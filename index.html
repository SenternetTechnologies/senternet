About Us

AR Rahman Constructions is a leading construction company based in Nagapattinam, Tamil Nadu, India. With years of experience, we provide comprehensive construction services tailored to your vision. We specialize in delivering turnkey house construction projects, innovative interior and exterior designs, and structural fabrication. Our commitment to quality, using the best materials and practices, ensures that your building stands the test of time. You dream it, we build it!

We have built over 50 residential and commercial projects, focusing on sustainable building practices and modern architecture. Our team consists of experienced engineers, architects, and skilled laborers dedicated to delivering projects on time and within budget.

Our Services

House Construction

Experience seamless house construction with our comprehensive turnkey services, ensuring every detail is expertly managed. Quality homes tailored to your vision. We handle everything from foundation to finishing, using high-quality materials like reinforced concrete and eco-friendly bricks.

Our process includes site evaluation, blueprint design, permits, and final handover, with a focus on energy-efficient buildings.

Interior Design

Transform your home with our innovative design solutions. We offer the best craftsmanship, premium quality, and endless design options. From modular kitchens to luxurious living rooms, we incorporate modern trends like smart home integration and sustainable interiors.

We collaborate with top suppliers for furniture, lighting, and decor to create spaces that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing.

Exterior & Elevation

Custom solutions for stunning visual aesthetics, enhancing curb appeal with beauty and durability. We design elevations that blend traditional Tamil architecture with contemporary styles, using materials like stone cladding and weather-resistant paints.

Our exterior services include landscaping, facade design, and outdoor lighting to make your property stand out.

Structural Fabrication

Durable solutions tailored to your construction needs, blending functionality with architectural precision. We specialize in steel and metal fabrication for roofs, gates, and supports, ensuring structural integrity for high-rise and industrial buildings.

Using advanced welding techniques and corrosion-resistant materials, we deliver robust structures that meet international safety standards.
